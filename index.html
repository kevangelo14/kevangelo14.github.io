About MaxiBuild

MaxiBuild Inc. was established and incorporated in August 1989 under laws of the Republic of the Philippines. As a contracting firm, MaxiBuild was organized to provide its clients with the following fields of expertise:

• General Building Construction
• Interior Fit-out Construction
• Project and Construction Management

Mission and Vision

MaxiBuild will be an organization efficient in the utilization of resources:

• Time
• Materials
• Manpower
• Machinery
• Money

MaxiBuild will be an organization well-respected in the construction industry. It will be known as a best-value contractor, integral to the success of its clients.
